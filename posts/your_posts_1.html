Public transportation is very important for all countries. The progress of a country can be seen from its transportation. Many people use motorcycle rather than public transportation. It is because they feel uncomfortable when using public transportation. Lacks of facilities is the main reason why people did not use public transportation.

UITP (International Association of Public Transport) says that people that use public transportation in Austria grew at a relatively high pace since 2003, by over 2% per year. The country had the third highest level of public transport in 2014. It is because public transportation in Austria was more comfortable than other countries.

All people in developing countries must try to use public transportation. Now, many developing countries trying to fix the facilities and learn from developed countries. If developing countries successfully fixed their public transportation, there is a chance to change those countries to developed countries.
